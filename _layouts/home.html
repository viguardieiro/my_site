About

Hi! I am currently a research scientist at the Visualization and Data Analytics Research Center (VIDA) at New York University (NYU), under the supervision of professor Claudio Silva.

In 2023, I concluded my M.S. in Mathematical Modelling at the School of Applied Mathematics (EMAp) of Fundação Getulio Vargas (FGV). I was part of the Visual Data Science Lab (V-DS) and worked with Professors Jorge Poco and Marcos M. Raimundo (from Unicamp). I obtained my B.S. in Applied Mathematics from FGV/EMAp in 2020 also under the supervision of Jorge Poco and Marcos M. Raimundo.

My current research focuses on visualization systems for Explainable AI and Topological Data Analysis. I previously researched Multi-Objective Machine Learning and the development of Fair ML models.

As part of my professional life, I worked with Mathematical Optimization and Computer Vision. For more information about my background, visit my curriculum.
